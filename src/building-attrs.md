## 建筑物符号（building）的属性

##### building-fill `color`


默认值： #FFFFFF


The color of the buildings walls.
* * *

##### building-fill-opacity `float`


默认值： 1


The opacity of the building as a whole, including all walls.
* * *

##### building-height `expression`


默认值： 0


The height of the building in pixels.
* * *
